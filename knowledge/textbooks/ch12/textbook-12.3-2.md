## 12.3 分式的加减（第二课时）
例 3 计算下列各式: 

(1) $\frac{x + 2}{x - 2} -\frac{x - 2}{x + 2};$ (2) $\frac{1}{9a^2 + 6a + 1} +\frac{1}{3a + 1}.$ 

解：(1) $\frac{x + 2}{x - 2} -\frac{x - 2}{x + 2}$ $= \frac{(x + 2)^2}{(x + 2)(x - 2)} -\frac{(x - 2)^2}{(x + 2)(x - 2)}$ $= \frac{(x + 2)^2 - (x - 2)^2}{(x + 2)(x - 2)}$ $= \frac{x^2 + 4x + 4 - (x^2 - 4x + 4)}{(x + 2)(x - 2)}$ $= \frac{8x}{(x + 2)(x - 2)}.$ (2) $\frac{1}{9a^2 + 6a + 1} +\frac{1}{3a + 1}$ $= \frac{1}{(3a + 1)^2} +\frac{3a + 1}{(3a + 1)^2}$ 

$$
\begin{array}{r l} & = \frac {1 + (3 a + 1)}{(3 a + 1) ^ {2}} \\ & = \frac {3 a + 2}{(3 a + 1) ^ {2}}. \end{array}
$$

分式的混合运算，与数的混合运算类似。在进行分式的加、减、乘、除混合运算时，一般要按照运算顺序进行：先算乘除，后算加减；如果有括号，要先算括号内的。 

例 4 计算下列各式: 

$$
\left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{a - b} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{b - a}\right) \cdot \frac {a b}{a + b};
$$

$$
\left(\frac {x + 2}{x ^ {2} - 2 x} - \frac {x - 1}{x ^ {2} - 4 x + 4}\right) \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}}.
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r l} & \left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{a - b} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{b - a}\right) \cdot \frac {a b}{a + b} \\ & = \left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{a - b} - \frac {b ^ {2}}{a - b}\right) \cdot \frac {a b}{a + b} \\ & = \frac {a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}}{a - b} \cdot \frac {a b}{a + b} \\ & = \frac {(a - b) (a + b)}{a - b} \cdot \frac {a b}{a + b} \\ & = a b. \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r l} & \left(\frac {x + 2}{x ^ {2} - 2 x} - \frac {x - 1}{x ^ {2} - 4 x + 4}\right) \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}} \\ & = \left[ \frac {x + 2}{x (x - 2)} - \frac {x - 1}{(x - 2) ^ {2}} \right] \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}} \\ & = \left[ \frac {(x + 2) (x - 2)}{x (x - 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {x (x - 1)}{x (x - 2) ^ {2}} \right] \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}} \\ & = \frac {(x + 2) (x - 2) - x (x - 1)}{x (x - 2) ^ {2}} \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}} \\ & = \frac {x ^ {2} - 4 - x ^ {2} + x}{x (x - 2) ^ {2}} \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}} \\ & = \frac {x - 4}{x (x - 2) ^ {2}} \div \frac {x - 4}{x ^ {2}} \end{array} \tag {2}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {x - 4}{x (x - 2) ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {x ^ {2}}{x - 4} \\ = \frac {x}{(x - 2) ^ {2}}. \end{array}
$$
