
**原题**：计算：$\frac{x+2}{x-2}-\frac{x-2}{x+2}$。

**参考解答**：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{x+2}{x-2}-\frac{x-2}{x+2}
&= \frac{(x+2)^2-(x-2)^2}{(x-2)(x+2)} \\
&= \frac{(x^2+4x+4)-(x^2-4x+4)}{x^2-4} \\
&= \frac{8x}{x^2-4}.
\end{aligned}
$$

**原题**：计算：$\left(\frac{a^2}{a-b}+\frac{b^2}{b-a}\right)\cdot\frac{ab}{a+b}$。

**参考解答**：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(\frac{a^2}{a-b}+\frac{b^2}{b-a}\right)\cdot\frac{ab}{a+b}
&= \left(\frac{a^2}{a-b}-\frac{b^2}{a-b}\right)\cdot\frac{ab}{a+b} \\
&= \frac{a^2-b^2}{a-b}\cdot\frac{ab}{a+b} \\
&= \frac{(a-b)(a+b)}{a-b}\cdot\frac{ab}{a+b} \\
&= ab.
\end{aligned}
$$

**原题**：计算：$\left(\frac{x+2}{x^2-2x}-\frac{x-1}{x^2-4x+4}\right)\div\frac{x-4}{x^2}$。

**参考解答**：

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\left(\frac{x+2}{x^2-2x}-\frac{x-1}{x^2-4x+4}\right)\div\frac{x-4}{x^2} \\
&= \left[\frac{x+2}{x(x-2)}-\frac{x-1}{(x-2)^2}\right]\cdot\frac{x^2}{x-4} \\
&= \frac{(x+2)(x-2)-x(x-1)}{x(x-2)^2}\cdot\frac{x^2}{x-4} \\
&= \frac{x^2-4-x^2+x}{x(x-2)^2}\cdot\frac{x^2}{x-4} \\
&= \frac{x-4}{x(x-2)^2}\cdot\frac{x^2}{x-4} \\
&= \frac{x}{(x-2)^2}.
\end{aligned}
$$

**原题**：当 $a=-\dfrac{2}{5}$ 时，求 $\dfrac{1}{a+1}-\dfrac{a^2+6a+9}{a^2-1}\cdot\dfrac{a-1}{(a+3)(a+1)}$ 的值。

**参考解答**：

先化简：

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\frac{1}{a+1}-\frac{a^2+6a+9}{a^2-1}\cdot\frac{a-1}{(a+3)(a+1)} \\
&= \frac{1}{a+1}-\frac{(a+3)^2}{(a-1)(a+1)}\cdot\frac{a-1}{(a+3)(a+1)} \\
&= \frac{1}{a+1}-\frac{a+3}{(a+1)^2} \\
&= \frac{a+1}{(a+1)^2}-\frac{a+3}{(a+1)^2} \\
&= \frac{-2}{(a+1)^2}.
\end{aligned}
$$

将 $a=-\dfrac{2}{5}$ 代入：

$$
\frac{-2}{\left(-\dfrac{2}{5}+1\right)^2}=\frac{-2}{\left(\dfrac{3}{5}\right)^2}=\frac{-2}{\dfrac{9}{25}}=-\frac{50}{9}.
$$

\left(\frac{a}{a+1}+1\right) &= \frac{a}{a+1}+\frac{a+1}{a+1} = \frac{2a+1}{a+1}, \\
\left(1-\frac{3a^2}{1-a^2}\right) &= 1+\frac{3a^2}{a^2-1} = \frac{a^2-1+3a^2}{a^2-1} = \frac{4a^2-1}{a^2-1} = \frac{(2a-1)(2a+1)}{(a-1)(a+1)}.
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\left(\frac{a}{a+1}+1\right)\div\left(1-\frac{3a^2}{1-a^2}\right) \\
&= \frac{2a+1}{a+1}\cdot\frac{(a-1)(a+1)}{(2a-1)(2a+1)} \\
&= \frac{a-1}{2a-1}.
\end{aligned}
$$

**原题**：当 $x=-3,\ y=2$ 时，求 $\left(x+\dfrac{xy}{x-y}\right)\div\dfrac{x^3}{x^2-y^2}-\dfrac{1}{x}$ 的值。

**参考解答**：

先化简：

$$
\begin{aligned}
x+\frac{xy}{x-y}
&= \frac{x(x-y)}{x-y}+\frac{xy}{x-y} \\
&= \frac{x^2-xy+xy}{x-y} = \frac{x^2}{x-y}.
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\left(x+\frac{xy}{x-y}\right)\div\frac{x^3}{x^2-y^2}-\frac{1}{x} \\
&= \frac{x^2}{x-y}\cdot\frac{x^2-y^2}{x^3}-\frac{1}{x} \\
&= \frac{x^2}{x-y}\cdot\frac{(x-y)(x+y)}{x^3}-\frac{1}{x} \\
&= \frac{x+y}{x}-\frac{1}{x} \\
&= \frac{x+y-1}{x}.
\end{aligned}
$$

将 $x=-3,\ y=2$ 代入：

$$
\frac{-3+2-1}{-3} = \frac{-2}{-3} = \frac{2}{3}.
$$
