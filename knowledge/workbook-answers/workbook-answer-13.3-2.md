# 13.3 е…Ёзӯүдёүи§’еҪўзҡ„еҲӨе®ҡ(дәҢ)

1. (1)C (2)D (3)B (4)D

2. (1) $AD = AE$ (2) $\triangle ADF, \triangle BCE, \mathrm{SAS}$ (3) $82^{\circ}$ (4)4

3. иҜҒжҳҺпјҡвҲөABвҲҘCDпјҢ
вҲҙвҲ BAE=вҲ DCF.
еңЁв–іABE е’Ңв–іCDF дёӯпјҢ
вҲө $\left\{\begin{aligned}AB&=CD,\\ \angle BAE&=\angle DCF,\\ AE&=CF,\end{aligned}\right.$ вҲҙв–іABEвүҢв–іCDF(SAS).

4. иҜҒжҳҺпјҡвҲөAB//CDпјҢ
вҲҙвҲ BAC=вҲ ECDгҖӮ
еңЁв–іBAC е’Ңв–іECD дёӯпјҢ
$\left\{\begin{array}{l} AB=CE, \\ \angle BAC=\angle ECD, \\ AC=CD,\end{array}\right.$
$\therefore \triangle BAC \cong \triangle ECD (\text{SAS}).$
$\therefore BC=ED.$

5. (1) и§Ј: $CD = BE$ .

зҗҶз”ұ: $\because \triangle ABC$ жҳҜзӯүиҫ№дёүи§’еҪў, $\therefore AC = CB, \angle A = \angle BCE = 60^\circ$ .

еңЁ $\triangle ADC$ е’Ң $\triangle CEB$ дёӯ, $\begin{cases} AC = CB, \\ \angle A = \angle BCE, \\ AD = CE, \end{cases}$ $\therefore \triangle ADC \cong \triangle CEB(SAS)$ . $\therefore CD = BE$ .

(2) иҜҒжҳҺ: $\because \triangle ABC$ жҳҜзӯүиҫ№дёүи§’еҪў, $\therefore AB = BC = AC$ .

еҸҲ $\because AD = CE$ , $\therefore AD - AB = CE - AC$ , еҚі $DB = EA$ . $\because \angle ABC = \angle BAC$ , $\therefore 180^\circ - \angle ABC = 180^\circ + \angle BAC$ ,

еҚі $\angle DBC = \angle EAB$ еңЁв–іDBCе’Ңв–іEABдёӯпјҢ $\begin{cases} DB = EA,\\ \angle DBC = \angle EAB,\\ BC = AB, \end{cases}$ $\therefore \triangle DBC\cong \triangle EAB(SAS),$ $\therefore \angle BDQ = \angle BEA.$ $\because \angle DBQ = \angle EBA,$ $\therefore \angle DQB = \angle EAB = 180^{\circ} - \angle BAC = 120^{\circ}.$ $\therefore \angle CQE = 180^{\circ} - 120^{\circ} = 60^{\circ}.$

6. и§ЈпјҡвҲөO жҳҜ $AA'$ пјҢ $BB'$ зҡ„дёӯзӮ№пјҢ
вҲҙ $AO = A'O$ пјҢ $BO = B'O$ .
еңЁв–іOAB е’Ң в–іOA'B' дёӯпјҢ $\left\{\begin{aligned}AO&=A'O,\\ \angle AOB&=\angle A'OB',\\ BO&=B'O,\end{aligned}\right.$ вҲҙв–іOABвүҢв–іOA'B'(SAS).
вҲҙ AB = A'B'.
