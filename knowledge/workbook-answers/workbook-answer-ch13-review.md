4. 证明：∵AE=BF，
∴AE+EF=BF+EF，即AF=BE.
在△ADF和△BCE中， $\left\{\begin{aligned}AD&=BC,\\ \angle A&=\angle B,\\ AF&=BE,\end{aligned}\right.$ $\therefore \triangle ADF \cong \triangle BCE(SAS).$ $\therefore \angle D = \angle C.$

5. (1)证明：∵△ABC是等腰直角三角形，
∴AC=CB，∠ACB=90°.
∴∠ACD+∠ECB=90°。
又∵∠ACD+∠DAC=90°，
∴∠DAC=∠ECB。
在△ADC和△CEB中， $\left\{\begin{array}{l}\angle ADC=\angle CEB,\\\angle DAC=\angle ECB,\\AC=CB,\end{array}\right.$ ∴△ADC≌△CEB(AAS).
(2)解：根据题意，得AD=4×6=24(cm),
BE=3×6=18(cm).

由(1)，得 $\triangle ADC \cong \triangle CEB$ ， $\therefore CE = AD = 24 \text{ cm}, DC = BE = 18 \text{ cm.}$ $\therefore DE = DC + CE = 42 (\text{cm})$ 。

6. 证明：如图，延长 $CD$ 到点 $G$ ，使 $DG = BE$ ，连接 $AG$ .

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/sporeplant/math-resource-engine@main/knowledge/images/93a4e7fe890e116e368ac1a2ee13ee926e5e455f5ca08dddae8f31a3dd871660.jpg)

∵四边形ABCD为正方形，
∴AB=AD，∠B=∠ADC=∠BAD=90°.
∴∠ADG=90°=∠B.
在△ABE和△ADG中，
$\left\{\begin{array}{l} AB=AD, \\ \angle B=\angle ADG, \\ BE=DG,\end{array}\right.$
$\therefore \triangle ABE \cong \triangle ADG(SAS).$
$\therefore AE=AG, \angle BAE=\angle DAG.$
$\because \angle EAF=45^\circ$,
$\therefore \angle GAF=\angle DAG+\angle DAF=\angle BAE+ $
$\angle DAF=\angle BAD-\angle EAF=90^\circ-45^\circ=45^\circ.$
$\therefore \angle EAF=\angle GAF.$
在△AEF和△AGF中，
$\left\{\begin{array}{l} AE=AG, \\ \angle EAF=\angle GAF, \\ AF=AF,\end{array}\right.$
$\therefore \triangle AEF \cong \triangle AGF(SAS).$
$\therefore EF=GF.$
$\because GF=DG+DF=BE+DF$,
$\therefore BE+DF=EF.$

7. (1) 证明：∵D 是 AB 的中点，
∴AD=BD.
又∵AC=BC，CD=CD，
∴△ACD≌△BCD(SSS).

$\therefore \angle ACD = \angle BCD = 45^{\circ}, \angle CAD = \angle CBD = 45^{\circ}.$ $\therefore \angle CAE = \angle BCG.$ $\because BF \perp CE,$ $\therefore \angle BFC = 90^{\circ}.$ $\therefore \angle CBG + \angle BCF = 90^{\circ}.$ 又 $\because \angle ACE + \angle BCF = 90^{\circ},$ $\therefore \angle ACE = \angle CBG.$ 又 $\because AC = BC,$ $\therefore \triangle AEC \cong \triangle CGB (\text{ASA}).$ $\therefore AE = CG.$ (2) $CM = BE.$ 证明：由(1)，知 $\angle ADC = 90^{\circ}$ , $\therefore \angle BEC + \angle MCH = 90^{\circ}.$ $\because CH \perp HM,$ $\therefore \angle CHM = 90^{\circ}.$ $\therefore \angle CMA + \angle MCH = 90^{\circ}.$ $\therefore \angle CMA = \angle BEC.$ 由(1)，知 $\angle ACM = \angle CBE = 45^{\circ}.$ 在 $\triangle CAM$ 和 $\triangle BCE$ 中， $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \angle CMA = \angle BEC, \\ \angle ACM = \angle CBE, \\ CA = BC, \end{array} \right.$ $\therefore \triangle CAM \cong \triangle BCE (\text{AAS}).$ $\therefore CM = BE.$

8. 解：(1) $\because \angle CAD + \angle ACD = \angle CAD + \angle AOE = 90^{\circ}$ , $\therefore \angle ACD = \angle AOE.$ $\because \angle AOE = \angle BOD,$ $\therefore \angle BOD = \angle ACD.$ 又 $\because \angle BDO = \angle ADC = 90^{\circ}, AD = BD,$ $\therefore \triangle BDO \cong \triangle ADC(AAS).$ $\therefore BO = AC = 6.$
