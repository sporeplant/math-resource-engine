第1(8)题


证明：∵BD垂直平分AC， 

$$
\therefore A D = C D, A B = C B.
$$

$$
\text { 又 } \because B D = B D,
$$

$$
\therefore \triangle A D B \cong \triangle C D B (\_ ①).
$$

$$
\therefore \angle B A D = \angle B C D.
$$

$$
\because \angle B C D = \angle A D E,
$$

$$
\therefore \angle A D E = \angle D A B.
$$

$$
\therefore (\underline {{\quad}} ②).
$$

$$
\because A E \perp A C, B D \perp A C,
$$

$$
\therefore \angle E A C = \angle D O C = 9 0 ^ {\circ}.
$$

$$
\therefore A E \parallel B D.
$$

∴四边形 ABDE 是平行四边形. 

若以上解答过程正确，①，②处应分别为（） 

A. SAS, $DE = AB$ 

B. SSS, $DE \parallel AB$ 

C. SAS, DE//AB 

D. SSS, $DE = AB$
